Professional Real Estate Agent

Leticia Andrade became licensed as an Illinois Real Estate Broker in 2005 and has bilingual communication skills in English and Spanish. Born and raised in Chicago, Leticia has an in-depth knowledge of the many diverse neighborhoods in the Chicago market. Leticia’s strong work ethic has led her to have successful careers with State Farm Insurance, Countrywide Home Loans, and JP Morgan Chase Bank. She continues to build on her success as a highly respected professional in the Chicago real estate market. She is actively involved with the Women’s Council of Realtors, Young Professionals Network, National Association of Hispanic Real Estate Professionals, and Realtors to the Rescue and has achieved certification as an Accredited Buyers Representative, Short Sales and Foreclosure Resource.

Upon embarking on her professional career in the banking and lending industries, she quickly discovered her affinity for real estate through the purchase of her first home at the age of 23. Leticia possesses passion, determination, boundless energy, and the lending background to guide her clients through a complex process. Leticia understands that the real estate market can be intimidating for some, but her experience helps bring confidence to the table that, in conjunction with her warm demeanor, puts clients at ease throughout the process. The purchase of one’s home is often one of life’s most important decisions and Leticia’s clients appreciate her compassionate and ethical approach. Leticia’s financial background and her dedication to keeping clients informed throughout the process sets her apart in a competitive industry.

As the mother of two amazing daughters, Leticia is devoted to their education and athletic activities. Leticia attended Barat College in Lake Forest, IL where she was a theater major and has since developed a passion for mentoring young women who aspire to build their careers. Leticia maintains her energy and enthusiasm through her dedication to fitness, including participation in the Warriors Running Group and with Push Fitness.
